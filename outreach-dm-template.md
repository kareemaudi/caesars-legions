## Key Principles

1. **Personalization:** Always reference something specific they posted/said
2. **Brevity:** 50-100 words max (people are busy)
3. **Social Proof:** Use response rate stats (15-20% vs 1-3% industry avg)
4. **Low Friction:** Make it easy to say yes (15 min, no commitment)
5. **Value First:** Offer demo/early access before asking for money

---

## Target Signals (Who to DM)

**High Priority:**
- Posted about cold email struggles in last 30 days
- B2B SaaS founder with >$10K MRR (check bio/pinned posts)
- Recently hired or looking to hire SDRs
- Posted about low conversion rates on outbound

**Medium Priority:**
- Active in SaaS communities (r/SaaS, Indie Hackers)
- Selling to other businesses (B2B)
- Has budget (raised funding or profitable)
- Open to trying new tools (posts about tech stack)

**Avoid:**
- B2C companies (wrong fit)
- Already using competitor successfully (satisfied)
- Too early stage (no revenue = no budget)
- Enterprise (too big, slow sales cycle)
