## Demo Flow (15-20 min)

### 1. Login Experience (30 sec)
- Show clean dark+gold login page
- Login as demo@mubyn.com
- Notice: smooth transition, auto-redirect to chat

### 2. Caesar Chat — The AI CEO (2 min)
- Type: "Tell me about my business finances"
- Caesar responds with context-aware guidance
- Show how chat is the command center — all roads lead to Caesar

### 3. CFO — Financial Intelligence (3 min)
- Navigate to CFO in sidebar
- Click "Generate Financial Analysis"
- GPT-4o creates:
  - Revenue/expenses/profit KPIs
  - 6-month projections with charts
  - AI-generated strategic insights
  - Break-even, CAC, burn rate, runway
- Show transaction management (add income/expense)

### 4. Leads — AI Sales Agent (3 min)
- Navigate to Leads
- Select Industry: Restaurants, Location: Dubai
- Click "Find Leads"
- GPT-4o discovers 10 real businesses:
  - Contact names, emails (guessed from website patterns)
  - Lead scoring (hot/warm/cold)
- Click a lead → Generate personalized cold email
- Show 3-email sequence (initial + 2 follow-ups)

### 5. CMO — Marketing Intelligence (3 min)
- Navigate to CMO
- **Overview Tab** (default): Analytics dashboard
  - Revenue chart (from Shopify if connected)
  - Ad spend + ROAS (from Meta/Google Ads if connected)
  - KPI cards: impressions, clicks, conversions, revenue
  - "Connect your Shopify store to see real data"
- **Content Tab**: Content calendar
  - Setup: Restaurant industry, English
  - Click "Generate Calendar"
  - GPT-4o creates 4-week content plan (12 posts)
  - Click "Generate Image" → DALL-E 3 creates branded visual
- **Campaigns Tab**: Campaign tracking
  - Placeholder for future campaign management
- Position: "Your marketing command center — analytics + content + campaigns"

### 6. CS Agent — Customer Support (2 min)
- Navigate to Customer Service → Test Agent tab
- Type customer question: "What are your opening hours?"
- AI responds naturally, using business context
- Show: Knowledge Base tab (add FAQs, product info)
- Show: Widget Setup tab (embed code for any website)
- **Show: Channels tab** — 5 channels supported:
  - Website Widget (embed anywhere)
  - Email (IMAP/SMTP — AI monitors inbox, auto-responds)
  - Telegram Bot (connect via @BotFather token)
  - WhatsApp Business (API webhook)
  - Live Chat fallback
- Show: Tone & Style (professional/friendly, language, response length)
- Position: "One AI agent, every channel"

### 7. Website Builder — AI-Generated Sites (2 min)
- Navigate to Website
- Click "Generate Website" (pre-filled from business profile)
- GPT-4o creates complete, premium website in ~30 seconds:
  - Glassmorphism design, gradient hero, scroll animations
  - Social proof, features grid, testimonials, pricing
  - Full Arabic RTL support if business language is Arabic
  - WhatsApp floating button, contact form
- Show preview in iframe
- Click "Edit" → describe changes in natural language → AI updates
- Click "Publish" → live at subdomain

### 8. Integrations — Connect Everything (1 min)
- Navigate to Settings → Integrations
- Show Shopify, Meta Ads, Google Ads cards
- Explain: "One-click OAuth coming. For now, API token connect."
- Data flows into CMO Analytics dashboard
- Position: "Your entire business, one view"
