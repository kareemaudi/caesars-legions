Hidden Costs of Human SDRs

  • Turnover: Average SDR tenure is 14 months. Recruiting and training is expensive.
  • Inconsistency: Performance varies day-to-day, week-to-week.
  • Sick days & vacation: 15-20 non-productive days per year.
  • Morale management: Cold calling is mentally taxing. Burnout is real.

Where Humans Still Win

  • Phone calls: AI can't replace a real conversation (yet).
  • Complex deals: Enterprise sales need human judgment.
  • Relationship building: Networking, conferences, referrals.
  • Creative problem-solving: Unusual situations need human intuition.

Where AI Dominates

  • Volume: 5-10x more outreach at the same cost.
  • Consistency: Every email goes out on time, every time.
  • Data-driven optimization: Continuous A/B testing at scale.
  • Initial outreach: The grunt work of cold email.

üë§ When to Choose Human SDRs

  • Enterprise sales ($100K+ deals): The relationship-building justifies the cost.
  • Phone-heavy sales: If cold calling is your primary channel.
  • Complex products: If explaining value requires real-time conversation.
  • High-touch industries: Healthcare, government, financial services.
  • You have the budget: $100K+/year per rep, fully loaded.

ü§ñ When to Choose AI SDR

  • SMB/mid-market sales ($5K-$50K deals): Volume matters more than white-glove.
  • Email-first outreach: If cold email is your primary channel.
  • Limited budget: Get enterprise-level outreach on startup budgets.
  • Testing new markets: Validate before committing to headcount.
  • Need to scale fast: No time for 3-month hiring cycles.
  • Startups & small teams: Founders can't afford dedicated SDRs.

  • Human touch where it actually matters
