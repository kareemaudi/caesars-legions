1. Your List Quality Matters More Than Your Copy

3. Follow-Ups Are Where Money Lives

Most responses come from follow-ups 2-5, not the initial email.

- Email 2: 10-15% open rate, ~2% response
- Email 3: 12-18% open rate, ~3% response
- Email 4-5: Where most conversations start

AI advantage: Never forgets. Perfect timing. Adjusts based on engagement signals.

Every campaign teaches you about:

AI advantage: Learns from every send. A/B tests automatically. Optimizes in real-time.

What Most Founders Get Wrong

1. Sending too early
They spray & pray before validating the list. Result: burned domains, spam flags.

2. Too much about them, not enough about prospect
"We're the leading..." ‚Äî prospect doesn't care. Start with their problem.

3. Asking for too much
Don't ask for a 30-min call in email 1. Ask for a simple reply or offer value first.

4. No testing framework
They send the same email 100 times. No learning = no improvement.

5. Giving up too early
Most stop after 2 follow-ups. That's where the gold is.

Real Talk: When AI Cold Email Doesn't Work

AI isn't magic. It fails when:

  • Your ICP is too broad (targeting "all SaaS founders")
  • Your offer isn't clear (what problem do you solve?)
  • Your market is too small (<100 total prospects)
  • You need complex enterprise sales (multi-stakeholder)
  • Your product requires deep demos before interest

Best for: SMB/mid-market B2B SaaS with clear value prop and definable ICP.
